Discover The Beautiful World

Kullu Manali


If you want to turn your face towards another wonderful hill station surrounded by natural significance, then Kullu Manali is the real deal. It is located on the banks of river Beas. Over the years, it has been one of the preferred destinations among tourists all over the world. Here you can enjoy the beauty of real nature in the form of vivid landscape, apple orchards, lush green and snow wrapped mountains, etc.


Day 1


Delhi - Manali

To reach the world-famous destination or say hill station, you have to reach Delhi first and then drive to Manali. After reaching Manali, our tour representative will pick up you and transfer to the pre-booked hotel & Check-in. Here you will enjoy delicious local cuisines in dinner and Overnight stay here in between the beautiful landscape.


Day 2


Sightseeing at Manali

After morning breakfast at the hotel, get ready to enjoy the wonderful Manali by visiting various ancient temples, like Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple, Tibetan Monasteries, and famous Vashit village. Well, afternoon time is free for your own leisure activities. Overnight stay at the hotel while eating mouth-watering dinner.


Day 3


Excursion to Manali

The next 3rd day is again a full-day excursion to Manali where you have a chance to see various world-famous places like Gulaba Marhi, Rohtang Pass, Solang Valley, Kothi Gorge, etc. Return to the hotel in the evening and enjoy a tasty dinner with full comfort overnight stay.


Day 4


Manali - Manikaran via Kullu - Manali

Take morning breakfast and check out from the hotel in Manali and head towards Manikaran, around 75 kms from Kullu. Manikaran is a popular religious and pilgrimage place of Hindus. This place is believed to be the sacred place where Lord Manu recreated human life when several floods destroyed everything. Here you have a chance to explore various temples and gurudwara. Well, after sightseeing, Manikaran moves towards Kullu for a half-day tour. In the evening, get back to the hotel and enjoy an overnight stay with a delightful dinner.


Day 5


Manali to Delhi Departure

After early morning breakfast, check out from the hotel and go back to Delhi for your onward journey, which will bring an end to your memorable Kullu Manali tour package by Travelogy India.
